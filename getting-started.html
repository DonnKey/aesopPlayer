1. Requirements

You need a smartphone or a tablet ("device") running Android, preferably Android 5.0 or newer.
You can also run the app on Android 4.2+ and the Kindle Fire tablets but there will be some limitations when making a dedicated player.

The Aesop Player has features for making a dedicated player, one that will do nothing but be an audiobook player. That's completely optional and unless you set that up it's just like any other application.

An Internet connection is needed during installation but it is not necessary when listening to audiobooks. See Hardware Considerations for more on hardware requirements, particularly if you are making a dedicated player.

2. Install Aesop Player

Open the Play Store app on your device, search for "Aesop Player" and tap "Install". It will ask for various permissions (so it can access books and adjust its settings). Grant them.

If your device doesn't have the Google Play Store (e.g. Kindle Fire), you can download the Aesop installation APK file directly from https://goo.gl/XJhKX7. Open the downloaded file on your device to install.

3. Open Aesop Player

Text-to-Speech engine selection.

The first time you open Aesop Player you may be prompted to choose a Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine. Choose one (Google is a safe bet - this can be changed later) and tap "Always".

Text-to-Speech is used by Aesop Player to read audiobook titles. If it doesn't work, check the FAQ for help.

4. Play with the app

Aesop will prompt you to download sample audiobook excerpts from within the app so that you can start testing it right away. These are short and will only take a few moments to download and install.

Play, pause, swipe left and right to change books, test flip-to-stop and other features. Settings is entered by tapping the "gear" icon. There is a setting (disabled when first installed) to make it hard to accidentally enter settings. See the Controlling Unintended Access to Settings for details. See Configuation Guide for other configuration information.

Flip to stop playback.

5. Install audiobooks to the device

There are a number of ways to install audiobooks on Aesop. Some involve the use of a computer, some involve using the Internet, and there are other ways. As an example, here's a way that doesn't require anything but your device and an Internet connection. There's a detailed discussion of other alternatives here. (If you have a very slow or expensive Internet connection, look at one of the other alternatives, audiobooks tend to be fairly large.)

  • Choose a free (no DRM) Internet audiobook provider as suggested in the FAQ, or anywhere you happen to know of. For now, choose one that downloads whole books as zip files, which is most of them.
  • Start your favorite Web browser on the device and navigate to that provider. Pick a book you want to read and download it. For most browsers, take the defaults, but in any case download the zip file into the Android default Downloads directory.
  • Return to Aesop, then go to Settings (the "gear" icon) and click on the New Books button along the bottom edge of the Settings screen.
  • You will be presented with a list of candidates that look roughly like book downloads to Aesop. Among them you should find the book you downloaded. Aesop will have tried to figure out the proper title and will display it. (If you have a lot of clutter in Downloads, be patient while Aesop digs through the clutter.)
  • Click on the checkbox to the left of the entry for your new book, and then, at the upper right, the INSTALL button. (For now, be careful not to check on any of the clutter.)
  • The Aesop player will display the names of the subfiles in the zip file as it unpacks them. (Sometimes the zip file has them "in order", sometimes not. It doesn't matter.) The entry will disappear from Downloads. (See here for why.)
  • Return to the main Aesop screen and navigate (swipe left or right) to your new book.
